Plants that Strive in the Sunlight

These plants require a moderate - high amoount of sunlight in order to grow. Some plants, like perennials and annuals can be both sun and shade plants. These "sun" plants often require plenty of water. A majority of flowers and plants will take water once a week. These plants need water once or twice a week.
